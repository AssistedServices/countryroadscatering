Accessibility Statement

We’re committed to making our website accessible and usable for everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines.

Measures we take

  • Responsive design and accessible color contrast.
  • Semantic HTML with landmarks, headings, and lists.
  • Keyboard‑navigable menus and interactive elements.
  • Meaningful link text and alt text for images.
  • Focus styles visible for keyboard users.
  • Reduced‑motion support where appropriate.

Compatibility

Our site is designed to work with the latest versions of major browsers (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari) on modern devices. Some older browsers or assistive technologies may not fully support all features.

Technical specifications

This website relies on HTML, CSS, and a small amount of JavaScript. No audio/video content is required to access key information.

Feedback & contact

If you experience any accessibility barriers, please let us know and we’ll do our best to fix them promptly.

Contact: hello@countryroadscatering.co.uk

Known limitations

We regularly review the site. If an issue slips through (e.g., missing alt text or focus order problem), we’ll prioritise a fix as soon as it’s reported.

Assessment approach

We use internal reviews and real‑world testing on common devices to check accessibility. We welcome third‑party feedback and audits.
