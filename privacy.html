Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Country Roads Catering (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website or contact us about catering services.

Who we are

Country Roads Catering — South Coast, UK.
Contact: michael@assist.wtf

What data we collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone) when you enquire or request our menu PDF.
  • Event information (date, venue, guest count, dietary needs) you share with us.
  • Technical data like IP address, device, and basic analytics (aggregated/anonymous where possible).
  • Communications you send us (emails, forms) and our responses.

How we use your data

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare quotes/menus.
  • To deliver our services and manage event logistics.
  • To send the requested full menu PDF and service updates (only if you opt in).
  • To maintain our website’s security and improve our content.

Legal bases

  • Contract – to provide quotes and fulfil catering services.
  • Consent – for optional updates/marketing. You can withdraw any time.
  • Legitimate interests – website security, preventing fraud, and improving services.

Sharing your data

We do not sell your data. We may share it with trusted service providers (e.g., website hosting, email delivery) strictly to operate our business. These providers must keep your data secure and only process it according to our instructions.

Storage & security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data. We keep it only as long as necessary for the purposes described, including legal/accounting requirements.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise any rights, contact michael@assist.wtf.

Cookies & analytics

Our site uses essential cookies for basic functionality. If we enable analytics, it will be configured to respect privacy (IP anonymisation where available). You can set your browser to block non‑essential cookies.

Links to other websites

Our website may contain links to third‑party sites. We’re not responsible for their privacy practices—please review their policies.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted here with the “last updated” date.
